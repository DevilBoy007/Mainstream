@antonywinfrey

Antony (Silky Love) Winfrey, is a talented singer and musician hailing from Indianapolis, Indiana, who captivates audiences with his soulful vocals and electrifying performances. With a passion for music that runs deep in his veins, Antony brings a unique blend of R&B, pop, and soul to the stage and your ears. His heartfelt lyrics and powerful melodies resonate with listeners, leaving a lasting impact on all who hear his music. Antony's dedication to his craft and his ability to connect with people sets him apart as a rising star in the music industry. Be prepared to be moved and inspired by Antony's silky music.

Kenna Loren is a singer local to Indianapolis. She performs in groups such as Mainstream, 2000 & Late and It's Just Business. Former lead singer for rock pop group Kenna & The Kings, she has written two albums and is working on her third as a solo artist.

@james_iv_

Baby James Loynes IV is a talented drummer hailing from Indianapolis with a passion for music that runs deep in his veins. From a young age, he found his rhythm and groove playing drums in the church, where he honed his skills and developed a unique style all his own. With a contagious energy and undeniable talent, Baby James has captivated audiences far and wide with his dynamic drumming performances. Whether he's playing in a band or jamming solo, his passion for music shines through in every beat. With a bright future ahead, Baby James continues to make his mark on the music scene in Indianapolis and beyond.
